# WST 157 · Chapter 2: Research & Universal Usability

This folder contains a static, classroom-ready presentation website for WST 157 Website Planning & Production.

## Open

Open `index.html` in a browser.

## Controls

- Next / Back buttons move through the deck.
- Arrow keys move forward and backward.
- Press `F` or click `Present` to hide the top bar and side route.
- Click `Overview` to jump to any slide.

1. Opening question
2. Main idea: best practices are not enough

8. Research method selector

13. Wireframes and prototypes
14. Mini usability test
15. Review quiz
16. Exit ticket
